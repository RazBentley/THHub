## Full Description (Both Stores)

TH Hub is your all-in-one personal training and nutrition companion, built by TH Training.

PERSONALISED MEAL PLANS
- Follow your coach-set daily meal plan with easy tick-off checkboxes
- Track water intake with visual progress
- Get free calorie allowances and supplement reminders
- See your daily and weekly adherence at a glance

NUTRITION TRACKING
- Search from thousands of foods including UK supermarket products
- Scan barcodes to instantly log packaged foods
- Track calories, protein, carbs, and fat with visual progress bars
- Add custom foods manually from product labels

DIRECT COACHING
- Message your coach directly through the app
- Share progress photos and updates
- Get real-time feedback and support

WEEKLY CHECK-INS
- Structured weekly check-in forms
- Track weight, sleep, energy, training, and adherence
- Set goals and reflect on your wins
- Automatic reminders on your check-in day

SIMPLE PAYMENTS
- Easy subscription management
- Secure payments powered by Stripe

Built for TH Training clients. Download now and take your training to the next level.
