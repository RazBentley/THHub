TH Hub - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

TH Hub ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal training and nutrition coaching application operated by TH Training. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use TH Hub, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Health & Nutrition Data: Food logs, meal plan progress, water intake, and weekly check-in information you voluntarily provide
  • Body Metrics: Weight and body measurements you submit through check-in forms
  • Messages: Text messages and images sent through the in-app chat feature
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your card details
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens for sending reminders

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalised nutrition and training coaching services
  • Display your meal plans and track your daily progress
  • Enable communication between you and your coach
  • Send check-in reminders and notifications
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve the app and our coaching services

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit. Our servers are located in Europe (London region).

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, and notifications
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing
  • Open Food Facts / USDA FoodData Central / FatSecret: Food and nutrition data lookup

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data is shared only with:

  • Your assigned coach/trainer (for coaching purposes)
  • Payment processor (Stripe) for subscription management

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or other third parties.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@thtraining.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

9. Children's Privacy

TH Hub is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
